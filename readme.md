To roll something with "advantage" or "disadvantage", simply enter the dice formula you wish to roll into the Dice field and add "+Na" or "-Nb" where "N" is the number of levels of advantage or disadvantage respectively which are applied to the roll, enter a custom name into the Label field if you'd like, and then click on the Roll button to load the dice into the tray. For each level of advantage or disadvantage applied to the roll, an additional dice is added to the largest positive dice being rolled and only the highest individual dice results from among those dice are kept. The omitted individual dice results are listed in the name of the roll once it is made, both in the displayed result and in the log.

**Disclaimer: All other players must have this symbiote running in order to see the properly calculated results.**
